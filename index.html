03

Before you join

Requirements

  • Minecraft Java Edition or Bedrock Edition, latest release
  • A Discord account, to reach staff and see announcements
  • Read the rules pinned in #rules on Discord
  • Complete the verification process before being whitelisted to join the server

House rules

  • No griefing or cheating
  • Keep chat respectful, no harassment or toxicity
  • No hacked clients, Freecam, minimap mods, Replay Mod for locating bases, or seed cracking
  • Use common sense and don't ruin the experience for others/li>

Join the community

Get your whitelist, chat with other players, and hear about events first. Everything happens on Discord.
